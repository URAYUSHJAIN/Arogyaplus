# Arogyaplus

Welcome to Arogyaplus! We're excited to announce that our platform is currently under construction. Our mission is to provide you with a comprehensive health and wellness solution that empowers you to take control of your health. Here's a sneak peek of what you can expect:

## Main Features

### Scheme Finder
- Discover various health schemes tailored to your needs.
- Get detailed information about benefits, eligibility, and application processes.

### Detection & Check-up through Symptoms
- Input your symptoms to receive personalized health advice.
- Get recommendations for check-ups and further diagnostics.

### Risk Assessment of a Disease
- Evaluate your risk for various diseases based on your health profile.
- Receive actionable insights to mitigate potential health risks.

### Custom Dietary Planner
- Create personalized diet plans based on your health goals and dietary preferences.
- Access nutritional information and meal suggestions to support your wellness journey.

### AI Query Resolver
- Get instant answers to your health-related queries using our advanced AI.
- Receive reliable and up-to-date information to address your health concerns.
